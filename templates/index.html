Our Mission

We at SmrutiTime facilitate young Balaks and Balikas' minds and hearts to develop by providing a vibrant forum to share and preserve their Smrutis (memories). We wish to encourage personal growth, spiritual reflection, and firm tradition bonding, and have built in a community of interest that encourages and inspires each other. We believe in the power of memories and narration in constructing the future while honoring our rich cultural heritage.

What We Do

SmrutiTime is more than just an app, it's a space where young minds can create, share, and reflect on their Smrutis. We provide a digital journal for personal reflections, as well as an interactive community page where users can share their memories with others. Through the practice of Anatradrashti (spiritual reflection), we encourage Balaks and Balikas to deepen their spiritual connection and reflection on their experiences. With each Smruti shared, we create a tapestry of experiences that not only preserve our past but inspire future generations.

Why It Matters

In a fast-paced world where we often forget to meditate on God's form and reflect on our spiritual journey, SmrutiTime offers a meaningful way to reconnect. By remembering and reflecting on past Smrutis, we cultivate a deeper sense of devotion and mindfulness. This platform not only enhances the practice of spiritual reflection but also shapes the future of BAPS, helping young Balaks and Balikas value their heritage and pass it on to the next generation. Through shared memories and experiences, we strengthen our collective bond and preserve the traditions that define us.
